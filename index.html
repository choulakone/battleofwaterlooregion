ABOUT

Waterloo Region is a Canadian region located in southern Ontario. It is composed of three cities: Kitchener, Waterloo, and Cambridge. It is also made up of four townships: Wellesley, Wilmot, Woolwich, and North Dumfries.

The Region of Waterloo has a population of 507,096 as of this writing.

Though it is made up of several cities and townships it sometimes feel like one big city. That's what makes this area unique.

The region is not perfect but it's growing. There's lots of job opportunities in the technology and insurance industry. The education system is strong, private and public sectors are investing in the area, and it's a great place to raise a family.
