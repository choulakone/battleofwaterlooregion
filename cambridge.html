City of Cambridge

ABOUT

Historically, Cambridge is an amalgamation of the City of Galt, the towns of Hespeler and Preston, and the hamlet of Blair.

Cambridge has this tranquile vibe to it.

Although, it sometimes doesn't get the same fame as Kitchener and Waterloo. People of Cambridge are frienlier and easy going.

There are some great architects. The Grand River runs through the city which gives it an amazing atmostphere. There are some nice restaurants that are worth visiting.
