Townships of Waterloo Region

ABOUT

The four townships are composed of small towns. Here you will find the country side of the region. It's a different lifestyle if you drive through the townships. It's a different pace.

If you want to learn more about produce, legumes, meats, dairy, and so on North Dumfries is the place to be.

Township of Woolwich is famous for Elmira's Maple Syrup Festival, St-Jacobs Farmer's Market, and is where the Waterloo Region Airport is located.

The Township of Wellesley is famous for their Apple cider and Apple Butter Festival.

Every fall visit the Shantz Family Farm located in the Township of Wilmot where you'll enjoy straw maze, wagon rides, and pumpkins.
