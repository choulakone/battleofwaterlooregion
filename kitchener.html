City of Kitchener

ABOUT

Kitchener was originally known as Berlin before 1916. It was later renamed to Kitchener due to the World War. Today, it is a multicutural city reflecting the mosaic cultures of Canada.

Throughout the year, Kitchener hosts several festivals including its Blues Festival, a two day Multicultural Day Festival, and many others.

Enjoy Kitchener's Farmer's Market located downtown Kitchener every Saturday.

There's some great landscape here in Kitchener. Victoria Park is its most well known park. Rockway Gardens is a beautiful place to take wedding pictures or read a book.
